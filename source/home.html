Model Polytechnic College Vadakara

The Model Polytechnic College, Vadakara is the brain child of Intitute of Human Resources Development (IHRD) established by the Govt. of Kerala in the year 1988, whose main objective is to function as a catalyst to foster the growth of electronics, computer and specialised feilds such as Medical Electronics throw a plethora of innovative endeavours.

The Polytechnic College offers three year Diploma cource in Bio Medical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Hardware Engineering, Electronics Engineering and Electrical and Electronics Engineering courses being recognised by the PSC. The institution has been accredited by the National Board of Aaccreditation (NBA) of the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi.

Model Polytechnic College Vadakara is a new generation Polytechnic offering courses in emerging technologies. The institution is managed by Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD) fully owned and controlled by Govt. of Kerala. College is located in NH-17 midway between Kannur and Calicut cities. This institution has a proven track record of academic excellence among the other polytechnic colleges in Kerala. Admission is fully based on merit and as per the Govt. rules. Model Polytechnic College Vadakara is affiliated to Board of Technical Education, Kerala and approved by Government of kerala.
