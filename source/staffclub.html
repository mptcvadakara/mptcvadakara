Staff Club

Our Purpose

A staff club is a social, cultural, and recreational forum for college and university faculty and staff to interact, promote a positive work environment, and foster a sense of community. These clubs organize various activities like cultural events, sports, and social get-togethers, often including celebrations of festivals, and may also address work-related issues or support staff welfare. Their activities contribute to work-life balance and can include professional development, felicitating achievements, and welcoming new members.

Functions and activities

  • Social and cultural events: Organize social get-togethers, celebrate festivals, and host cultural programs and annual events for staff and their families.
  • Recreational activities: Arrange sports competitions, games, and tours.
  • Professional development: Conduct expert talks, workshops, and training sessions to help staff enhance both their hard and soft skills.
  • Welfare and support: Provide a platform for discussion, offer support to staff and their families, and assist new members with things like finding accommodation.
  • Recognition and farewells: Felicitate staff members for academic and other achievements and organize farewell functions for those who retire.
  • Community outreach: Participate in outreach and charity programs, such as visiting old age homes or collecting and distributing food and clothes.

Membership & Committee

Membership is open to all permanent and temporary faculty and staff members of the college. The club is governed by an elected executive committee that oversees its operations and finances.
