In the beginning of the polytechnic we had a Medical Electronics branch, and from 2015 onwards, the branch was renamed as Biomedical Engineering, with a modification in the syllabus. In this diploma course, students will gain a strong base in electronics. They study Basic Electronics, Electrical Engg., Electronic circuits, Linear integrated circuits, Digital Electronics, Communication Engg., etc. After that, the syllabus includes various imaging technologies, Therapeutic equipments, diagnostic equips, analytical equipments, and basics of hardware and networking. Candidates completing this course can work as biomedical engineers / service technicians in hospitals or biomedical equipment agencies.

Objectives:

  • Apply engineering principles to healthcare.
  • Design modern diagnostic and therapeutic equipment.
  • Train students in analytical and research technologies.
  • Improve healthcare standards through innovation.
Department Vision Department Mission
  • To be a center of excellence in Biomedical Engineering education and research.
  • To develop skilled professionals for the healthcare industry.
  • To promote innovation in medical technology.
  • To contribute towards improved healthcare systems.
  • Provide strong foundation in electronics and biomedical systems.
  • Equip students with practical and technical skills.
  • Encourage research and industry collaboration.
  • Develop ethical and socially responsible engineers.
